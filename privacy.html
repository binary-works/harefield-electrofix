Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Data We Collect

This website does not collect any personal data through forms. Customers contact us directly via WhatsApp using the phone number provided on the site. Any information shared via WhatsApp is stored only in our WhatsApp app on a secure phone and email mailbox.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use contact information only to respond to enquiries about small electrical repairs (sockets, plugs, lighting fixtures) and manage communications. We retain certain client data for legal and tax compliance purposes, including possible checks by HMRC.

3. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Your consent when contacting us via WhatsApp.
  • Legal obligations to retain data for tax and regulatory compliance.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in password-protected WhatsApp on phone and email mailbox, accessible only to authorised personnel. Appropriate technical and organisational measures protect your personal data.

5. Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to respond to enquiries or as required by law for tax purposes (typically at least 5-7 years for HMRC compliance). After this period, records will be deleted.

6. Third-party Services

This site may use privacy-respecting analytics (no cookies, no personal data collection) to measure traffic. We may link to generic third-party services in future but take no responsibility for their privacy practices.

7. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (except where legally required to retain)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact us at electrofix-info@protonmail.com to exercise your rights.

9. Contact

For questions about this policy, email electrofix-info@protonmail.com.
